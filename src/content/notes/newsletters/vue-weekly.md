# Vue Weekly #2 — January 3-10, 2026

## TL;DR

> **This week:** The Vue ecosystem accelerates into 2026 with Vapor Mode progress in Vue 3.6 beta, Vite 8 previewing the Rolldown bundler, and Nuxt Studio going free and open-source — all while the community prepares for Nuxt 3's end-of-life on January 31st.

**Must-read:**

- **Nuxt Studio goes FREE and open-source** — The visual editing platform for Nuxt Content sites is now accessible to everyone
- **Vue 3.6.0-beta.2** — Continued progress on Vapor Mode, Vue's upcoming compile-time optimization strategy
- **Vite 8.0.0-beta.7** — First look at the Rolldown bundler integration, promising significant build performance gains
- **Nuxt 3 EOL: January 31, 2026** — Only 3 weeks left to migrate to Nuxt 4; start planning now
- **Vue hits 6.4M weekly npm downloads** — 37% year-over-year growth shows strong ecosystem momentum

**Week theme:** _Transition and Transformation_ — This week marks a pivotal moment as the ecosystem evolves: Vue approaches Vapor Mode, Vite embraces Rolldown, Nuxt Studio democratizes content editing, and teams worldwide prepare for the Nuxt 4 migration deadline.
