## Overview

Slam Dunk follows Hanamichi Sakuragi, a delinquent who joins Shohoku High School's basketball team to impress a girl, only to discover a genuine passion for the sport. The series chronicles the team's growth and their pursuit of the national championship.

## Why Read This

One of the most influential sports manga ever created, credited with popularizing basketball in Japan. Combines intense sports action with comedy and character development. The art evolved dramatically throughout the series, showcasing Inoue's growth as an artist.

## Notable

- Sold over 170 million copies worldwide
- Ran in Weekly Shonen Jump from 1990-1996
- Adapted into an anime series and the 2022 film _The First Slam Dunk_, directed by Inoue himself
