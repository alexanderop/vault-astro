# Vue Weekly #2

_Jan 03 - Jan 10, 2026 | Your weekly dose of Vue ecosystem news_

## TL;DR

> **This week:** The Vue ecosystem accelerates toward its next major chapter - Vue 3.6 beta advances Vapor Mode, Vite 8 integrates Rolldown for faster builds, Nuxt Studio goes fully open-source, and teams have 3 weeks to migrate before Nuxt 3 EOL.

**Must-read:**

- [Nuxt Studio is now FREE and open-source](https://content.nuxt.com/blog/studio-oss) - Major shift from premium to self-hosted OSS
- [Vue 3.6.0-beta.2](https://github.com/vuejs/core/releases/tag/v3.6.0-beta.2) - Vapor Mode and alien-signals reactivity refactor
- [Vite 8.0.0-beta.7](https://github.com/vitejs/vite/releases/tag/v8.0.0-beta.7) - Rolldown bundler integration progresses
- **Nuxt 3 EOL: January 31, 2026** - 3 weeks to migrate to Nuxt 4
- Vue hits **6.4M weekly npm downloads** (+37% YoY growth)

**Week theme:** _Transition and Transformation_ - Nearly every major story involves the ecosystem evolving to its next iteration.

## Releases

### Vue.js

- **[v3.6.0-beta.2](https://github.com/vuejs/core/releases/tag/v3.6.0-beta.2)** (Jan 4) - Vapor Mode and major @vue/reactivity refactor based on alien-signals. _The Vapor Mode hype train keeps rolling._

### Vite

- **[v8.0.0-beta.7](https://github.com/vitejs/vite/releases/tag/v8.0.0-beta.7)** (Jan 8) - Latest beta with Rolldown bundler integration.
- **[v7.3.1](https://github.com/vitejs/vite/releases/tag/v7.3.1)** (Jan 7) - Stable patch for production users on v7.x.

### Nitro

- **[v2.13.0](https://github.com/nitrojs/nitro/releases/tag/v2.13.0)** (Jan 8) - Vercel skew protection enhancements and Bun idle timeout support.

## Official Announcements

- **[Nuxt Studio is Now Free and Open Source](https://content.nuxt.com/blog/studio-oss)** (Jan 5) - The content management platform transitions to a self-hosted, open-source Nuxt module. _Big win for content-heavy Nuxt projects._
- **[Nuxt Studio 1.0](https://nuxt.studio)** (Jan 7) - Official 1.0 launch following the open-source announcement.
- **[Vite 8 Beta](https://vite.dev/blog/announcing-vite8-beta)** (Dec 3) - Rolldown bundler promises significant compilation speed improvements.
